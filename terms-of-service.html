üì± Service Description

2.1 What is Findrer?

Findrer is a Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) device finder application designed for iOS devices. The Service helps users locate their misplaced Bluetooth-enabled devices through:

  • Real-time proximity detection using RSSI (Received Signal Strength Indicator)
  • Visual and audio feedback to guide users to their devices
  • Last-known location mapping (Premium feature)
  • Lost/found notifications (Premium feature)
  • Custom device naming and organization

2.2 How Findrer Works

The App scans for nearby Bluetooth devices using your iPhone's Bluetooth radio. It calculates approximate distance based on signal strength and displays devices in order of proximity. The App does not connect to your devices; it only detects their Bluetooth advertising signals.

Technical Note: Findrer uses standard iOS CoreBluetooth framework and does not require pairing or connecting to your devices.

6.1 Bluetooth Technology Limitations

Findrer relies on Bluetooth Low Energy technology, which has inherent limitations:

Limitation Description
Range Typical range: 10-30 meters (33-100 feet) in open air. Range is significantly reduced by walls, furniture, and obstacles.
Accuracy Distance calculations have ¬±2-5 meter (¬±6-16 feet) error margin. Accuracy varies by environment.
Detection Device must be powered on and have Bluetooth enabled to be detected.
Interference Metal objects, water, concrete, and other electronic devices can interfere with Bluetooth signals.
Background Scanning iOS limits background Bluetooth scanning to approximately 10 seconds when app is not active.

6.2 Location Services Limitations

The map view feature (Premium) relies on GPS location services:

  • GPS Accuracy: Typical accuracy is ¬±10 meters (¬±33 feet) but can be worse indoors or in urban canyons
  • Last-Known Location: Shows where your device was last detected, not its current location if it has moved
  • Search Radius: The displayed ~30-meter radius circle is an approximate search area
  • Indoor Limitations: GPS accuracy degrades significantly inside buildings

6.3 Device Compatibility

Not all features work with all devices:

  • Sound Playback: Only works with devices that support remote audio trigger commands (e.g., AirPods, some speakers)
  • Device Names: Some devices broadcast generic names (e.g., "Unknown Device") that cannot be customized by the manufacturer
  • Stealth Mode: Some devices can operate in non-discoverable mode and won't appear in scans

6.4 No Guarantees

Important Disclaimer: Findrer is a tool to assist in locating Bluetooth devices but does not guarantee you will find your lost device. Success depends on many factors including battery life, Bluetooth range, environmental conditions, and whether the device is powered on.
