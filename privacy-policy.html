Welcome to Findr! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Findr mobile application ("App").

Findr is designed to help you locate your Bluetooth devices using proximity detection and location services. We believe in transparency and want you to understand exactly what data we collect and why.

Privacy First: Your privacy is our top priority. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. All data collected is used solely to provide and improve the App's functionality.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

The App automatically collects certain information to function properly:

  • Bluetooth Information: We scan for nearby Bluetooth devices to detect their presence, signal strength (RSSI), and proximity. This includes device identifiers, names, and connection status.
  • Location Data: When you grant permission, we collect your device's GPS coordinates to:
    • Save the last known location of tracked devices
    • Display device locations on a map
    • Enable location sharing features

  • Device Information: We collect basic device information such as:
    • iOS version
    • App version

2.3 Purchase Information

If you purchase premium features, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your credit card information or payment details. We only receive confirmation of purchase status through Apple's StoreKit framework.

Important: All data collected by Findr is stored locally on your device. We do not operate remote servers or upload your data to the cloud.

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Device Tracking: To detect and track Bluetooth devices in your vicinity
  • Proximity Detection: To calculate distance and provide audio/visual feedback
  • Location Mapping: To show device locations on a map (Premium feature)

üîí Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure UserDefaults system. This includes:

  • Custom device names
  • Device marking preferences (My Devices)
  • Notification settings
  • Last known device locations
  • Premium purchase status

4.2 Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you:

  • Manually delete the App
  • Clear app data through iOS Settings
  • Reset your device

4.3 Security Measures

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted Storage: All data is stored using iOS's encrypted storage mechanisms
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data stays on your device and is never transmitted to external servers
  • Secure Bluetooth: Bluetooth scanning uses Apple's Core Bluetooth framework with built-in security
  • Permission-Based Access: Location and Bluetooth access require explicit user permission
Data Protection: Since all data is stored locally, you have complete control. We cannot access, view, or recover your data remotely.

ü§ù Third-Party Services

5.1 Apple Services

Findr uses the following Apple frameworks and services:

  • Core Bluetooth: For scanning and detecting Bluetooth devices
  • Core Location: For GPS location tracking (with your permission)
  • StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases (Apple handles all payment data)
  • UserNotifications: For sending local notifications about lost/found devices
  • MapKit: For displaying device locations on maps

These are native Apple frameworks that respect iOS privacy policies. No data is shared with Apple beyond standard App Store analytics (which you can disable in iOS Settings).

5.2 No Third-Party Analytics

We do NOT use any third-party analytics services such as:

  • Google Analytics
  • Facebook SDK
  • Crashlytics
  • Firebase
  • Any other tracking services
Zero Third-Party Data Sharing: Your data is never shared with, sold to, or transmitted to any third-party companies or services.
